The Designer
"Every stitch is an act of love. Every woman deserves to feel like herself — but elevated."

GeeGee Designs was born in Tlokweng, Botswana, out of a passion for making women feel extraordinary. What started as first fittings with friends quickly grew into a beloved brand, with clients traveling from Goodhope, Francistown, and South Africa to experience the magic of a custom-made piece.

Every design is made-to-measure. Every fabric is chosen with intention. Every woman who walks out of the studio walks out glowing.

— GeeGee, Founder & Designer
